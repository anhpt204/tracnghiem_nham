This package provides functionality to simulate consumption and charging of electric vehicles in MATSim. Vehicles' energy consumption is calculated on a link-to-link basis. Charging may take place at facilities.

For examples and use cases, consult also

  • Waraich, R.A. (2013) Agent-based simulation of electric vehicles: Design and implementation of a framework, Dissertation, IVT, ETH Zurich, Zurich. Download
  • Bischoff, J. & Maciejewski, M. (2014) Agent-based simulation of electric taxicab fleets,Transportation Research Procedia, Presented at MobilTUM'14, 2014, 4, 191-198 Download

    • For a code example, see org.matsim.contrib.transEnergySim.example.car.RunTransEnergySimExample.
